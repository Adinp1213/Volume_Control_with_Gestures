About Us

Welcome to Smart Volume Control, a revolutionary application that allows you to control your system's volume using hand gestures.

Our application uses computer vision and machine learning technologies to detect hand gestures and adjust the volume accordingly. The system is designed to be intuitive and easy to use, providing a new and interactive way to control your audio settings.

Technologies Used:

  • Python
  • Django Framework
  • OpenCV
  • MediaPipe
  • pycaw
