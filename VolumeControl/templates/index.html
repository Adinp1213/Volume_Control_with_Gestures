Working of Hand Recognition Model:

Our Smart Volume Control uses a hand recognition model to interpret gestures for controlling the volume. When you raise a certain number of fingers, it adjusts the volume accordingly. Here's a brief overview:

Fist: Mute

1 finger: 25% volume

2 fingers: 50% volume

3 fingers: 75% volume

4 fingers: Max volume


Don't include thumb in finger calculation
